About Us

Strategy that survives contact with reality.

Northpeak Consulting was founded on a simple frustration: too many growth plans look great in a slide deck and fall apart in week two. We work differently — close to the ground, inside your numbers, honest about what's actually slowing you down.

Our team has spent the last decade inside operations, finance and go-to-market teams across a dozen industries. We bring that experience to bear on your specific situation, not a generic playbook. Every engagement ends with a plan you could execute without us — though most clients ask us to stay.

For a specific, high-stakes challenge — entering a market, fixing unit economics, restructuring a team. Scoped tightly, delivered fast.
